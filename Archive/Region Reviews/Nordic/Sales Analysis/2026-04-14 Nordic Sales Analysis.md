## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Post-transition demand dropped 35-42%.** Adib's claim of "no drop in sales" is not supported by the data. Kit DSR went from ~30/d to ~18-20/d. This needs investigation — is it a temporary disruption (broken flows, missing listings, confused customers) or structural demand loss?

2. **Remove 120ml is OOS in 3 days.** 29 units left, 8.9/d demand. No GLAMRDiP stock exists. This is the most urgent operational item.

3. **3 popular colours stock out this week/month.** Buttercup (9d), Bloom (12d), Angel Energy (31d). All top-15 sellers with small initial quantities from NORDIC 14012026.

4. **Dippi stock is being used to fulfil GLAMRDiP orders** for liquids and Remove products. This works but creates a confusing picture in the POS MODEL and will end when Dippi stock runs out — no replacement plan for several items.

5. **Base stocks out ~18 May with no replacement.** No local filler in Nordic, no confirmed container before July. This will halt standalone Base sales.

6. **Heal stocks out ~29 May at kit-adjusted rate.** All Dippi stock, no GLAMRDiP. When this runs out, kits can't be assembled.

7. **POS MODEL is non-functional for Nordic forecasting.** No DSR on GD SKUs, no shipment blocks, no 3PL data, uncorrected stock errors. This needs to be rebuilt.
