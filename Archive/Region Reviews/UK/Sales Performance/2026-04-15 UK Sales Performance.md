## KIT MIX ANALYSIS

| Kit | 14d % | 30d % | Model % | Shift | Read |
|-----|-------|-------|---------|-------|------|
| Starter | 17% | 17% | 12% | Stable | Over-indexed vs model — more entry-level buyers than expected |
| Complete | 31% | 31% | 44% | Stable | **Significantly under-indexed** — the middle option is underperforming |
| Ultimate | 53% | 52% | 44% | +1pp | Over-indexed — customers choosing premium |

**AOV proxy: ULT share at 53% (14d) vs 52% (30d) — increasing.** Customers are choosing the most expensive kit more than half the time. This is a strong commercial signal.

**Complete Kit gap:** The model assumes 44% Complete, but actual is 31%. This is a persistent structural gap, not a recent shift. Two possible reads:
1. **Pricing/value gap** — the jump from Starter (3 colours) to Complete (6 colours) doesn't feel worth it, so customers skip to Ultimate (9 colours)
2. **CRO opportunity** — the CRO team's GLMR-0017 experiment (Replace 2/4/6 to 3/6/9) is in discussion. If the middle option becomes 6 colours instead of 4, it may fill the gap

Either way, the ULT dominance is a positive AOV story. The model's 44/44 assumption for COM/ULT should be recalibrated to ~30/55 to reflect reality.

## COMMERCIAL SUMMARY

1. **UK is accelerating and hitting its growth target.** 7d kit DSR (93.7) just passed the 92.4 model target. This is the only region at or above target. The 8-week trajectory shows +34% organic growth (71.4→95.9) with promo bumps recovering to higher baselines each time.

2. **ULT dominance is a strong AOV signal.** 53% of kit sales are Ultimate — customers are choosing the premium option more than half the time. The model assumes 44%. This means average order value is structurally higher than the model predicts.

3. **Standalone colour sales add 28% volume above kit picks.** Customers are buying 9.1 colours per kit equivalent vs the 7.1 expected from kit mix. This standalone colour demand is incremental revenue. The GLMR-0012 sticky colour bar (launched 8 Apr) may be amplifying this.

4. **Remove 500ml is cannibalising 120ml.** The 120ml is fading (-40%) while 500ml is breaking out (+34%). If this is a genuine size shift, it's AOV-positive. Confirm 120ml isn't OOS on the website.

5. **Bouquet (POW-BOU-222) needs attention.** Was a top-15 seller at 13.6/d, now at 3.9/d — a 71% collapse. This isn't post-promo normalisation (the decline started before Easter ended). Check website listing status.
